8. Scenario description of Android sensitive permissions

Get contact information. Under the premise of obtaining the user's permission, it is limited to synchronizing the mobile phone contacts to the Bluetooth device, and the app will not privately collect and leak user privacy.

Get SMS. Under the premise of obtaining the user's permission, it is limited to forwarding the SMS information received by the mobile phone to the smart device, and the app will not collect and leak user privacy privately.

Get call history. Under the premise of obtaining the user's permission, it is limited to synchronizing the incoming and outgoing status of the mobile phone to the Bluetooth device, and the app will not privately collect and leak user privacy.

Get storage permissions. With the permission of the user , it is limited to the storage and reading of files such as pictures, OTA firmware, databases, dials, etc.

Get camera permissions. With the permission of the user , it is limited to remote photography, dial upgrade, QR code scanning, etc.

Get location permission. Under the premise of obtaining the user's permission, it is limited to ble scanning, motion function positioning, and weather status acquisition. The app will not collect privately and reveal user privacy.
