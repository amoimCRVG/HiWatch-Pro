The Device Firmware Update (DFU) app is compatible with Realsil Bee devices that support full LE features. It allows to upload new applications and image onto the device over-the-air (OTA).

  • Step 1：The DFU discovers DFU devices, connects to them.
  • Step 2：select firmware applications file(*.bin)
  • Step 3：uploads user selected firmware applications to the device
